Project Purpose

StudyPlanner was created with the vision to help students better manage their academic life. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive solution that helps students:

  • Organize their class schedules efficiently
  • Track assignments and deadlines effectively
  • Manage study sessions and improve productivity
  • Monitor academic progress through an intuitive dashboard

This project was developed as part of our BSc.IT curriculum, combining our technical skills with a real-world solution that benefits students.
